Components

Zeta Architecture

Zeta is our processor chip lineup. We calibrate these units specifically targeting each device:

  • Port: U/U+ series high-end computing with integrated graphics and neural engine.
  • Play: H/H+ series focusing solely on gaming or heavier-workload.
  • Station: X/X+ series monster performers.
  • Alphone: M/M+ series configured for mobile systems.

Instead of relying on ARM or x86 architecture, we developed ARM86. It acts as a conjugating bridge between both, and is available in all of our devices.

Theta Graphics

Theta is our graphical chip lineup specifically for heavy lifting:

  • Play: U/U+ series working with Zeta for gaming dominance.
  • Station: X/X+ series monster performers for rendering.

We are planning on entering the dedicated market with our theta chipsets partnering with third-party solutions soon.

UMG Technology

Our ARM86 architecture allows for Unified Memory Gigs (UMG). We created a bridge allowing RAM, VRAM, and Storage to work seamlessly.

If Theta requires more VRAM, it borrows from Storage. If the system is low on RAM, it borrows from VRAM. The possibilities are endless.

*Note: UMG is currently not open-source while we discuss the ethics of power abuse by other corporations.

Lambda OS

Our proprietary ecosystem is built upon the robust Arch Linux foundation, engineered specifically for the iRhZi hardware stack. It is designed to bridge the gap between extreme user customizability and seamless out-of-the-box performance.

  • Lambda DE: A bespoke Desktop Environment bridging the customizability of KDE Plasma with the focused, minimalist ergonomics of GNOME.
  • Kappa PM: Our ultra-fast, native package manager designed for software deployment, ensuring atomic updates and native support for the iRhZi ecosystem
  • The "Eta" Layer: A specialized, low-latency compatibility bridge (derived from Wine and Proton) that allows industry-standard gaming, creative, and office suites to run natively without performance degradation.
  • Hardware Synergy: Specifically optimized for Zeta CPUs and Theta GPUs, Lambda OS utilizes our ARM86 instruction set to achieve near-zero overhead in system tasks.
  • Ecosystem Integration: Deployed as Lambda-D for Deltop and Lambda-A for Alphone, providing a unified continuity experience across all hardware form factors.

The Zero-AI Initiative:

In accordance with our user-first philosophy, Lambda OS contains zero implementations of LLMs or Generative AI at the kernel or shell level. Your data remains local, private, and human-driven.
